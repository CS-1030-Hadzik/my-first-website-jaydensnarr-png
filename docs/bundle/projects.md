## SnowBidPro

<video class="project-video" autoplay muted loop playsinline>
<source src="/assets/images/SnowBidPro.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

SnowbidPro was designed to help solo operators
gather leads through a customer facing self served bidding software. An easy system that was designed to simplify the communication between operator and customers through an automated sms notifications based on the operator trigger input on the contractor dashboard. SnowBidPro actively identifies customers who have accepted their new bids or reservice notifications and automatically creates a route so the operator focuses on what they do best.
